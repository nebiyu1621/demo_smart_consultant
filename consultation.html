Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your project with us.

What happens after I submit a consultation request? expand_more
Once you submit your request, one of our senior project managers will review your details. You will receive an initial response within 24-48 business hours to schedule a deep-dive call or in-person meeting.
How long does it take to receive a formal project quote? expand_more
Depending on project complexity, a formal proposal and detailed quote typically take between 5 to 10 business days after the initial consultation call.
What service areas do you cover? expand_more
While we are headquartered in Addis Ababa, we serve clients across East Africa and handle international structural design consultations for projects worldwide.
Is SMART Consulting ISO certified? expand_more
Yes, we maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification for Quality Management Systems and follow all local and international building codes for structural integrity.
